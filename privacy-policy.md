# Privacy Policy for Fond Faces

**Last Updated: May 6, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Fond Faces ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We built Fond Faces with privacy as a core principle: your memories belong to you.

## 1. Your Photos and Videos Stay on Your Device

Fond Faces is designed to process your personal photos and create memoir timelapse videos **entirely on your device**. 

- **No Cloud Uploads:** We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos, videos, or the timelapses you create to our servers or any third-party servers.
- **On-Device Face Detection:** The App uses on-device machine learning (Google ML Kit) to detect faces in your photos. This processing happens locally on your phone. No facial recognition data or biometric information ever leaves your device.
- **Sharing:** Your content only leaves the App if you explicitly choose to share a created video using your device's built-in sharing mechanisms (e.g., sending via messages, email, or social media).

## 2. Information We Collect

While your personal content stays strictly on your device, we do collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how the App is used and to improve your experience.

**Anonymous Usage Analytics:**
We collect non-personally identifiable information about how you interact with the App. This may include:
- App launch frequency and session duration.
- Which features or screens are used most often.
- Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version).
- Crash reports and performance metrics.

**This analytics data NEVER includes your photos, videos, facial data, names, or any other personal content.** 

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to power specific features of the App:

- **Amplitude:** We use Amplitude to collect and analyze the anonymous usage data described above. You can read Amplitude's Privacy Policy at [https://amplitude.com/privacy](https://amplitude.com/privacy).
- **Google ML Kit:** We use Google ML Kit for face detection. As implemented in Fond Faces, this service runs entirely on-device and does not send your photos to Google.
- **App Stores (Google Play / Apple App Store):** If you make in-app purchases, your payment is processed directly by the respective app store. We do not collect or have access to your payment details (like credit card numbers).

## 4. Data Security

Because your photos and generated videos are processed and stored locally, their security relies on your device's security measures (such as your device passcode, biometric lock, and device encryption). We recommend keeping your device secured to protect your personal content.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Fond Faces does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. Since the App does not collect personal data or upload photos, children's content remains safely on their devices.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

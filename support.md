## Common Issues

### 1. Photos are not appearing in the app
- Confirm photo access is enabled in iOS Settings for Fond Faces.
- Re-open the app after granting permissions.

### 2. Video creation is not working
- Ensure the app has enough free device storage.
- Try reducing the number of selected photos.
- Force close and reopen the app, then try again.

### 3. Face detection seems incorrect
- Use clearer photos with visible faces.
- Open Face Alignment / review tools and manually confirm the correct face when needed.
